PETYA Ransomware

Many organizations throughout Europe and the US have been crippled by a ransomware attack known as “Petya”. The malicious software has spread through large firms including the advertiser WPP, food company Mondelez, French construction materials company Saint-Gobain and Russian steel and oil firms Evraz and Rosneft, legal firm DLA Piper, Danish shipping and transport firm Maersk and now targeting government and financial institutions, leading to PCs and data being locked up and held for ransom.
