May 14, 2018
HIGHGATE ANNOUNCES ITS ‘NEW’ IFRS 9 MODULE

Highgate is extremely excited to present to you our NEW! IFRS 9 Module. IFRS 9 is an International Financial Reporting Standard (IFRS) promulgated by the International Accounting Standards Board (IASB). It addresses the accounting for financial instruments.

IFRS 9 is the response from the International Accounting Standards Board (IASB) to the disruption of the global financial systems and networks that resulted from the failure of certain financial institutions and the near failure of critical industries. The new regulations are intended to improve the health of financial institutions through more appropriate classifications of impaired assets. Improved reporting provides regulators with tools intended to avoid systemic sector and geographic risk.

PETYA Ransomware

Many organizations throughout Europe and the US have been crippled by a ransomware attack known as “Petya”. The malicious software has spread through large firms including the advertiser WPP, food company Mondelez, French construction materials company Saint-Gobain and Russian steel and oil firms Evraz and Rosneft, legal firm DLA Piper, Danish shipping and transport firm Maersk and now targeting government and financial institutions, leading to PCs and data being locked up and held for ransom.

Oct 21, 2015
MASSY SIGNS WITH HIGHGATE!

We are pleased to announce that Massy Credit Plus Ltd. (Massy), which currently operates in Trinidad and Barbados, has selected our Highgate Banking and ESPLink ™ Suite of financial services products.

Our ESP-Link™ Suite of technology manages ATM terminals and provides transaction switching (acquiring) services for both domestic and international transactions. Along with Collection Management, the AMS product provides real-time SMS alerts for things such as customer transaction activity to registered smartphones and much more.

With this new technology, Massy Credit Plus Ltd. (Massy) will now be able to provide their customers with real-time loan and credit payments, decisions on credit and to communicate directly with their customers via SMS and much more.

We would like to welcome Massy as Highgate newest client and thank them for their confidence and commitment with respect to both our people and technology.
